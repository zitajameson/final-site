About

Hi again,

I am a junior at the University of Michigan School of Information with a concentration in Information Analysis (think data analytics!).

Through my experiences with Reach Consulting Group (RCG) I have affirmed my passion for working on data-driven solutions to help companies optimize their performance, effectiveness, and efficiency. When working with others I feel energized to solve technical challenges by noticing relevant patterns and issues at the intersection of people, data, and technology.

This coming summer I am joining KPMG as a Digital Lighthouse Digital Consultant Intern in the Philadelphia office!

Some other facts about me - I am a native of the incredibly unique Upper Peninsula of Michigan: a nature-filled, beautiful part of the United States that has remained a secret from the world. Additionally, I have had the privilege of obtaining my Private Pilot's Certificate and hope to continue my aviation education throughout my life.

Although I have loved living in the midwest and can not imagine living in a real city or farther than a few hours away from a Great Lake, I am eager to challenge myself and live outtside the Midwest!
